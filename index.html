Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products, manufacturing process, and services.

What makes Momsoul wet wipes eco-friendly?

Our eco-friendly wet wipes are made with sustainable materials like bamboo fiber, cotton, and soy fiber. We offer various blends including 100% Viscose and compostable options. By 2027, our goal is to have a fully biodegradable product line.

What is your minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

We offer the lowest MOQ in the market, making our services accessible to businesses of all sizes. The exact quantity varies by product type, but we pride ourselves on being flexible to accommodate your needs.

What certifications does Momsoul have?

We are ISO 9001:2015 certified, ensuring our quality management systems meet international standards. Our formulations are also lab-tested and certified, guaranteeing safety for consumers, homes, and the environment.

How does your manufacturing process work?

Our end-to-end manufacturing process begins with a consultation to understand your needs. We then help you select the product category, wipes material, thickness, strength, texture, sheet count, size, packaging type, lid, formulation, and active ingredients. After finalizing packaging design and copy, we move to production and quality control, delivering a market-ready product.

What countries do you ship to?

We ship globally to over 20 countries including Australia, Canada, China, Japan, Singapore, South Korea, and many more. Our Country of Origin Certification ensures traceability and authenticity, and FTA Partner Countries enjoy preferential benefits.
